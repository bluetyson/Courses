\paragraph{Entropy of a random variable $X$}
    
Definition:

$H(X) = \Sigma_{x_i} x_i * ( - \log \left(p(x_i)\right) )$

\paragraph{Properties of $H(X)$}

\begin{itemize}
    \item $ H(X) \le 0 $.
